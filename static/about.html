About Us

We are on a mission to make people’s lives easier and mealtime tastier.

We take a fresh approach to the food industry by delivering hygienic, delicious, better-for-you, homely meals, right to your door. We are here so that you can finally ditch all the grocery shopping, cutting, cooking and cleaning and take mealtime stress off your plate. Sprink enables you to have your daily meals without skimping on variety or great taste. All of it, brought to you on a platform that is wondrously easy to use and at no regret prices.

Say No To Kitchen & Yes to Life with the most Easy, Affordable food services for your daily meals.

Our Philosophy

Cook responsibly & deliver food fresh and tasty.

We love food, and we’re committed to making it the right way. All Sprink food has to meet our three-point code. First, it has to be healthy, made with trusted ingredients from trusted vendors. Second, it has to be prepared with utmost hygiene standards. Finally, it has to taste good.

ALL THE MEALS WE MAKE
Ingredients Sourced from Licensed Vendors

A meal is only as good as its ingredients. We mindfully source fresh veggies, hearty whole grains from farms and distributors who are highly trusted and licensed in the industry, so every bite oozes of fresh flavours.
No Frozen Ingredients

Fresh, Fresh and only Fresh vegetables, greens, and tender Halal chicken are used in cooking, so that the best side of Indian cuisine comes alive in every bite.
Real Cooking by Expert Chefs

Home made taste is always better. That’s why our meals are hand-crafted and hand cooked by expert chefs and taste-test everything for quality before plating and sending to dispatch.
Healthy Standardised Usage of Oils & Spices

Tasty does not mean extra oily and spicy. Our chefs know this, and all food is prepped with keeping this in the center.
All natural ingredients.

Oh — and we never use artificial ingredients or preservatives in our cooking. Period.

No Artificial colors or flavours.*
